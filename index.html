About Us

VARSHA FASHIONS, is about 25 year old manufacturing company. The Company was promoted by Mr N.Alagar, who had made Varsha to spread its wings in the entire length and breadth of Tamilnadu and neighboring states with its Brand PLATO. Mr. N. Alagar, is the founder chairman. From a humble beginning today VARSHA has become a force to reckon with in the readymade shirt industry in Tamilnadu.

Today Varsha has about 250 valuable cliental spread across Tamilnadu and neighboring states. It has its own manufacturing plant in Madurai, spanning to about 60000 sq ft with all latest State of Art Technology. It has a capacity to produce 3000 shirts per day and plans are afoot to diversify in to Children's wear and Ladies garments also.

QUALITY AND VALUE FOR MONEY is the WATCH WORD Varsha, which is assured through its manufacturing process that goes through 27 sections.

Manufacturing facility - Varsha manufactures Men's shirt both formal and casual of various sizes and designs with and without embroidery.

MARKETING - The Brand PLATO of Varsha is being sold through leading retail outlets spread across Tamilnadu. We sell directly and through reputed agents and today PLATO brand can be found in about 300 textile outlets, 50 Men's show room and 2 Varsha's own outlet.

Varsha is planning its own show room in all the leading districts of Tamilnadu, particularly in cities like Chennai, Madurai, Coimbatore, Trichy and Pondicherry.
